Not having a website means missing out on significant advantages:

1. Online Visibility: Without a website, you are invisible to the vast online audience actively searching for products, services, and information.

2. Credibility and Trust: A website establishes legitimacy, professionalism, and trustworthiness, vital factors in gaining customer confidence.

3. Customer Engagement: A website provides a platform to engage with potential customers, answer inquiries, and showcase your offerings effectively.

4. Competitive Edge: In today's digital landscape, competitors with websites have a distinct advantage over those without, capturing online market share.

5. Business Growth: A website enables expansion into new markets, drives sales, and acts as a powerful marketing tool, contributing to long-term business growth.

CLInnovIT offers a flexible pricing model to accommodate the needs and budgets of our clients. For simple static websites, our prices start from Rs. 4000, providing a cost-effective solution for businesses looking for a basic online presence. For more complex and responsive websites, our prices start from Rs. 10000 and may vary based on the specific requirements and functionalities desired.

Additionally, we provide website maintenance services on a subscription basis, starting from Rs. 1000 per month. These services ensure that your website remains up-to-date, secure, and optimized for optimal performance. The pricing for maintenance services may vary depending on the size and complexity of the website, as well as the frequency of updates required.

At CLInnovIT, we understand that each business has unique requirements and budgetary constraints. As such, we are open to negotiations to find a pricing solution that works best for you. Our aim is to deliver exceptional value while ensuring affordability and client satisfaction.

As per our standard clause, for any project undertaken by CLInnovIT, we require a 50% advance payment before commencing the work. This advance payment serves as a commitment to initiate the project and cover the initial costs associated with the development process. The remaining balance is due upon the completion of the project and before the final delivery of the website. We believe this payment structure ensures a fair and transparent process for both parties involved, enabling us to provide you with the best possible service and results.
